Eko Bond

Eko Bond is our low-cost solution for milk collection centres in the rural areas and at the village level. It integrates five different modules to provide a total solution based on ultrasonic analyzers, data processing, integrated printing and mobile telecom services like GPS and GPRS. With this unit comes the convenience of electronic data processing and transfers to and from remote locations. With this integrated solution, remote management of multiple collection centres and analyzers can be done easily. Data management under various categories like rate and price management, collection and analysis of accrued data, data on individual or group of vendors, quantum of milk collection can all be remotely administered and inspected with the data at your fingertips in real-time. What is more, real-time updates or upgrades of software can be done easily and remotely too.
